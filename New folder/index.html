Three Decades of Innovation

ThinkPad is a line of business-oriented laptops originally designed by IBM and sold to Lenovo in 2005. The industrial design was created in 1990 by Richard Sapper, based on the concept of a traditional Japanese bento lunchbox. Over the decades, ThinkPad laptops have traveled to outer space aboard the International Space Station and have earned a lasting reputation as one of the most reliable and iconic laptop lines in computing history.
